Figure 9. Microscopy Feature Correlation Screen. Quickly screen relationships across multiple morphology features before deeper modeling. The strongest morphology relationship is between Petal.Length and Petal.Width (r = 0.96), suggesting those measurements may be tracking overlapping biology. The Pearson correlation screen is the statistical shortcut here: it quickly tells you which features are likely redundant before you commit to a larger phenotyping panel. Source data: iris from the R datasets collection, framed here as a single-cell morphology feature-screen proxy.
